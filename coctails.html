Eye of the storm
Spiced ingredients come together beautifully for this exotic take on a classic mule.
Ingredients
  • 1 oz Zaya Rum
  • 1 oz Lemongrass-Infused Bourbon
    *or Bourbon w/ Lemongrass Bitters
  • 3/4 oz Lime Juice (more to taste)
  • Ginger Beer (dry & spicy)
  • 5 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Lime twist & tropical flower (for garnish)
How to craft
  1. Add ice to a copper mug
  2. Add Zaya Rum, bourbon & lime juice
  3. Fill with ginger beer
  4. Top with Angostura bitters
  5. Garnish with lime & flower
Sip & Sail
Utilize a dry shake to create a luxurious foam on this impressive cocktail.
Ingredients
  • 1 oz Zaya Rum
  • 1/2 oz White Rum
  • 3/4 oz Lime Juice
  • 2 Blackberries
  • 1 - 2 Raspberries
  • 1/2 oz Allspice Dram
  • 1/4 oz Simple Syrup (2:1)
  • 1 Egg White
  • Cinnamon, Berries & Torched Lime Round (for garnish)
How to craft
  1. In a cocktail shaker, add berries & muddle
  2. Add all other ingredients to shaker
  3. Dry shake vigorously with no ice
  4. Add ice & shake well again
  5. Double strain into a coupe
    *using both a Hawthorne & fine mesh
    strainer
  6. Dust with cinnamon
  7. Garnish with a raspberry & blackberry on a
    cocktail pick & a torched lime round
